Research Insights & FAQ.

How do you verify your B2B respondents?

Our B2B panelists undergo a rigorous multi-step verification, including LinkedIn profile matching, work email confirmation, and screening phone calls for high-incentive executive studies.

What is your typical turnaround time for global fieldwork?

Turnaround varies by niche, but standard consumer studies in Tier 1 markets usually complete within 3–5 business days. Niche B2B or Healthcare studies may take 10–14 days depending on the incidence rate.

Are you compliant with international data laws?

Yes. Clique Survey is fully compliant with GDPR (Europe), CCPA (California), and PIPA (Asia). We maintain ISO 20252 certification for market and social research.

Can you handle custom survey programming?

Absolutely. Our in-house scripting team handles everything from basic logical flows to complex max-diff, conjoint analysis, and custom shelf tests using Decipher and Confirmit.
